About Our System

A platform that brings lawyers and clients together for effective case management.

Key Features

Secure Login

JWT-based role authentication ensures that both clients and lawyers access only their respective data with full privacy and protection.

Case Management

Track all your legal cases, documents, hearing dates, and notes in one place for easy follow-up and management.

Smart Scheduling

Built-in calendar support helps schedule and manage appointments between lawyers and clients effortlessly.

Real-Time Updates

Stay informed with instant notifications for updates in your case status, meetings, or shared documents.
